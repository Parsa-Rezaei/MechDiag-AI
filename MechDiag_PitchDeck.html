The Problem

Diagnosing mechanical faults relies on scarce, specialized expertise. The current predictive maintenance paradigm faces critical bottlenecks:

  • Interpretation Complexity: Junior technicians struggle to cross-reference FFT spectra, phase readings, and defect frequencies manually.
  • Data Fragmentation: Engineers must manually correlate sensor data with dense, 500-page PDF service manuals.
  • Economic Impact: Misdiagnosed faults lead to premature part replacement, unexpected catastrophic failure, and millions in downtime.

The Solution: MechDiag AI

A virtual Senior Vibration Analyst powered by a Large Language Model agent capable of strict diagnostic reasoning.

Multi-Modal Ingestion

Agentic Reasoning

Routes data through logic trees (ISO 13374, ISO 20816) and identifies missing measurements instead of guessing.
